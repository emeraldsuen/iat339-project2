Branding Idea

What does the company do, or offer?

Magpul Industries Corporation is an American designer and manufacturer of high-tech polymer and compositre firearms accessories. It is founded in 1999 with the intent of developing a simple device to aid in the manipulation of rifle magazines while reloading under stress.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

The targeted customers are the military, police forces, private security, and professional gun owners.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Magpul is known for its creative design solutions, the simplicity and uniqueness of the design of its product provide an aesthetic look to their tactical gears and with efficient quality and performance.
